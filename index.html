Our Work

At the Seaside Restaurant and Bar in Crescent City, a dedicated team works tirelessly to provide customers with a truly unforgettable dining experience.

The kitchen staff prepares delectable dishes using homemade ingredients. The servers work efficiently to ensure that each guest feels welcomed and well-cared for, delivering hot plates of food and refilling drinks with a smile. Meanwhile, the host greets guests at the door and manages reservations, making sure that everyone has a place to sit and enjoy the stunning ocean views. Behind the scenes, the dishwashers keep the kitchen running smoothly, and the bussers clear tables to make room for the next group of diners. Every person at the Seaside Restaurant and Bar plays an important role in creating a welcoming and memorable atmosphere for all who visit.
